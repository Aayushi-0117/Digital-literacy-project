Social Media Do’s and Don’ts
Do’s
- Use respectful and professional language in posts and comments.
- Share academic achievements, projects, and constructive content.
- Verify information before posting or forwarding.
- Protect your privacy by limiting personal details shared online.
- Engage positively in discussions and support peers.
Don’ts
- Post offensive, discriminatory, or inappropriate content.
- Overshare personal information such as phone numbers or addresses.
- Spread rumors, fake news, or unverified information.
- Engage in online arguments or trolling.
- Upload photos or videos that could harm your reputation.
